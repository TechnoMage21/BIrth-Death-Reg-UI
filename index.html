I. IMPORTANT GENERAL INFORMATION

  • Birth or Death certificates can only be obtained if the event is registered with KMC.
  • Registration must be done within 1 year of the event.
  • Delayed registration is allowed only through the KMC Magistrate.
  • Events occurring within KMC's jurisdiction can only be registered by KMC.
  • The KMC area is divided into 15 boroughs, each with a designated Registration Unit.

II. PROCEDURE TO OBTAIN BIRTH CERTIFICATE

A. For Births in Private Hospitals/Nursing Homes

  • Birth certificates can be obtained from the Borough Ex. Health Officer after 6 weeks, subject to mandatory reporting by the hospital.
  • Name of the child can be inserted at the time of certificate issue.

B. For Births in Government Hospitals

  • Birth certificates can be obtained from the concerned Borough Health Office within 1 year on presentation of the medical certificate.
  • Name of the child can be inserted at the time of certificate issue.

C. For Domiciliary Births

  • Reported to the Ward Health Unit within 1 month but not later than 1 year with medical certificate.
  • Delayed registrations beyond 1 year can be processed on Magistrate's order.

III. PROCEDURE TO OBTAIN DEATH CERTIFICATE

A. For Deaths in Private Hospitals/Nursing Homes

  • Death events in KMC jurisdiction are registered at the Crematorium or Burial Ground, and certificates are issued on the spot.
  • Death events outside KMC jurisdiction can be registered at the KMC Main Office.
